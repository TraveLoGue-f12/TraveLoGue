Tari Kecak

Kecak Dance is regularly performed in many places all over Bali Island. However, the best place to watch this spectacular show is at the Pura Uluwatu, where the dance is performed daily with as background the dramatic sunset. The dance can also be found at Tanah Lot, GWK Cultural Park, Pura Dalem Ubud, Padang Tegal Stage, Batubulan, Umadewi Stage, and more.

Saung Angklung Mang Udjo

Located in the bustling eastern part of Bandung, Udjo's House of Angklung attempts to manifest beautiful harmony of vibrating bamboos that echoes from this simple amphitheater all the way to the other side of the world. It spreads the values of simplicity, co-operation, unity, and togetherness, as well as maintaining the mutual relationship between humans and the environment.

Handep

Handep is a leading social enterprise and sustainable brand in Indonesia that works with indigenous artisans and smallholders to create an alternative sustainable village economy through sustainable premium crafts. Our vision is to create a sustainable village economy that aligns with ancestral wisdom through local resources. We partner with indigenous communities, starting from Central Kalimantan and recently expanded to Pandeglang and Bali, to create high-quality designed products using local materials that are sourced from local forests and individual forest gardens as a way of sustainable economic development and cultural preservation. Through our products, services and way of working, we aim to not only support village communities and preserve the rainforests, but also foster a real human connection between the makers and customers.

Rou

ROU is a fashion and lifestyle Batik brand that is connecting modern lifestyle and tradition. ROU highly respect the values of Batik as one of Indonesian traditional culture. We believe Batik is a culture that is more than a set of rituals, but truly a way of living. It represents a philosophy full of positivity, hope and empowerment. Unfortunately, with the change of time, Batik has become a culture that is far from our everyday lifestyle. We become motivated to bring Batik to our daily life through the goods that closely match today's modern needs. So Batik can once again be a part of our way of living, not only seen during special occasions.

Bromo Mountain

This active volcano in East Java puts on a show every morning, when the glorious sun rises with the backdrop of the magnificent cone. Tourists can observe Bromo from the nearby Pananjakan, then head to Bromo itself and witness the beauty of its boiling crater.
